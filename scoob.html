Synopsis

When a young Scooby and Shaggy first meet, and team up with Daphne, Velma and Fred to launch Mystery Incorporated, they are soon on the way to becoming formidable crimebusters. Now, with hundreds of cases solved and adventures shared, Scooby and the gang face their biggest, most challenging mystery ever: a plot to unleash the ghost dog Cerberus upon the world. As they race to stop this global "dogpocalypse", the gang discovers that Scooby has a secret legacy and an epic destiny greater than anyone imagined.
