Dion Wired



Dion is one of South Africa's premier consumer electrical and home appliances concept stores. Operating in over 24 stores throughout South Africa, Dion Wired aims to give customers the optimal shopping experience at the most affordable prices.In addition, Dion Wired is a subsidiary of the JSE listed Massmart Holdings, the third largest distributor of consumer goods and also the leading retailer of general merchandise, liquor, home improvement equipment and supplies, and the leading wholesaler of basic foods in Africa.


The products offered at Dion Wired include TV and audio equipment, appliances, computers, laptops and computing equipment, mobile and smart technology products, games, consoles and gadgets, fridges, and photography and navigation equipment. They source the best products from around the world and show their customers how they can be used to simplify their lives.
